Bio

Born and raised in Emilia, I moved to The Netherlands to see if a piece of land can be more flat than the Pianura Padana (it can!). Although I bike in the rain like a true Hollander, my body keeps running on parmesan and tortellini, which I stock up regularly from the source.

I got a MSc in Systems and Control from TU Delft and now I come up with pieces of software that aim to make a better use of the existing electrical infrastructure. I do this at Spectral with tens of other committed and bright minds.

I find great pleasure in diving deep into entangled matters. When I don't get stuck myself, I enjoy crafting (hopefully) clearer but still accurate stories, and sharing them around. Humans, nature and technology seem to have a talent for creating complex scenarios, I am lucky! This space was born in the attempt of capturing and shaping this storytelling energy.
